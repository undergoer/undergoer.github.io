This is the "Get help" guide for the MHFA chatbot.

Once the page has loaded you will get the app that looks like the image below with the 3 annotations below.

  • red circle: you can restart by reloading the page by clicking this circular arrow
  • yellow ellipse: you can get back to this help page by clicking on these 3 vertical dots and selecting "Get help"
  • green box: you can assess your interaction with the chatbot by clicking on the "ALGEE Assess" button inside this box
